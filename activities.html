Math and Computer Science Club

Math and Computer Science Club is an academic club encouraging interest in Mathematics and Computer Science. We hold events to foster departmental unity, between faculty and students and between students. Math and Computer Science Club also offers math tutoring at Saint Mary's College and to local students. Hypatia Day is another annual community event that Math and Computer Science Club helps sponsor. Hypatia Day promotes interest in the STEM fields among local seventh grade girls. Other events include Math Movie Night, Weekly Math Contest, Math Department & Club gear, and alumnae presentations. Math and Computer Science Club also helps organize the Hooding Ceremony in the spring for graduating seniors.

Contact: mathclub@saintmarys.edu
